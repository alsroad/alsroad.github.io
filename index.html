Biography

Welcome to alsroad’s webpage.

I’m a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Seoul National University (Advisor: Prof. Taekyoung ‘‘Ted’’ Kwon).

My research topic lies on privacy. Currently, I’m interested in information flow control, privacy-preserving advertising, anonymous authentication, trusted execution environment, fully homomorphic encryption (FHE) and so on.
