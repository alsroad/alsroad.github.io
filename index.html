Biography

I’m currently working as a postdoctoral researcher at the Erik Jonsson School of Engineering and Computer Science, University of Texas at Dallas, USA.

My research focuses on preserving user privacy by analyzing software vulnerabilities and developing new privacy-preserving computation techniques. Specifically, I work in areas such as dynamic information flow control, fuzzing, program analysis, PKI/TLS, trusted execution environment (Intel SGX, Arm TrustZone), among others.
